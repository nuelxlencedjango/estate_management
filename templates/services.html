BPOs + CMAs

A BPO (broker price opinion) is a compilation of information about a property as well as the opinion of a real estate agent as to the value of said property. Many times, a BPO will include information about the home, such as the number of bedrooms and bathrooms, the square footage, the acreage and more.

A CMA (comparative market analysis) is similar to a BPO in that it provides an estimated value of the property. However, a CMA is much more focused on comparable properties and estimating a value based on properties that have sold recently.
